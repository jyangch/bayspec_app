Welcome to BaySpec

A browser-based spectral fitting workbench. Load OGIP-like spectra, compose multi-component models from local, Astromodels, XSPEC or user-defined libraries, then run a Bayesian sampler or maximum-likelihood optimizer — all without leaving your browser.

What you can do

Bring your data

Upload OGIP-format src / bkg / rsp (or rmf + arf). Configure fit statistic, notice energy, grouping, and rebinning per unit. Supports multi-wavelength observations.

Compose your model

Pick model components from local, Astromodels, or XSPEC libraries, or write your own in Python. Combine with + − × ÷ operators to build arbitrary composite models.

Run inference

Pair Data ↔ Model, manually adjust parameters and inspect the fit statistic, then launch a Bayesian sampler or ML optimizer. Posterior summaries, corner plots, and fitted spectra are rendered automatically.

Installation

BaySpec is available on PyPI:

pip install bayspec
